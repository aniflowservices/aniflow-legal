# Privacy Policy for AniFlow

**Last updated: March 2026**

---

## Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use AniFlow:

* **Account Information**: Email address and display name when you create an account
* **User Data**: Anime lists, folders, preferences, and app activity
* **Device & Usage Data**: Basic device information (such as device type, OS version) and app usage data for analytics and performance

---

## How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

* Provide and maintain the AniFlow service
* Sync and store your anime lists and preferences
* Improve app performance and user experience
* Send notifications you have opted into

---

## Advertising

AniFlow uses **Google AdMob** to display ads.

AdMob may collect and use data such as:

* Device identifiers
* Approximate location
* App interaction data

This data is used to provide **personalized and non-personalized ads**.

You can learn more here:
https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

---

## Third-Party Services

AniFlow relies on the following third-party services:

* **Firebase (Google)** – authentication, database, analytics, and app performance
  https://policies.google.com/privacy

* **AdMob (Google)** – advertising services
  https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

* **AniList** – anime data provider
  https://anilist.co/terms

These services may collect data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

---

## Data Sharing

We do **not sell your personal data**.

We only share data with third-party services listed above as necessary to operate the app.

---

## Data Retention & Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from:

**Settings → Delete Account**

Upon deletion, your data will be permanently removed from our systems.

---

## Children’s Privacy

AniFlow is not intended for children under 13.
We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

---

## Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data using secure technologies provided by Firebase and industry best practices.
