# Privacy Policy for AniFlow

Last updated: February 2026

## Information We Collect
We collect your email address and display name when you create an account. We store your anime lists, folders, and notification preferences.

## How We Use Your Information
Your data is used solely to provide the AniFlow service — storing your library and sending anime notifications you have opted into.

## Third Party Services
- Firebase (Google) — authentication and data storage. See Google's privacy policy at https://policies.google.com/privacy
- AniList — anime data provider. See AniList's terms at https://anilist.co/terms

## Data Deletion
You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from Settings → Delete Account.
