Our Story

Built by a fan, for fans

I'm 17, and I built AniFlow because I was tired of how messy anime tracking felt. Switching between apps, forgetting episodes, losing track of shows - it just wasn't it.

So I decided to build something myself. Clean. Fast. Actually useful. Every feature comes from a real problem I had as an anime fan.

AniFlow is just getting started - and I'm building it in public, one update at a time.
