🚀 Why I Built AniFlow

I’m 17, and I built AniFlow because I was tired of how messy anime tracking felt. Switching between apps, forgetting episodes, losing track of shows — it just wasn’t it.

So I decided to build something myself. Clean. Fast. Actually useful.

🔥 What Makes AniFlow Different

AniFlow isn’t just another tracker. It’s built around how people actually watch anime.

  • 📺 Smart episode tracking
  • ⚡ Fast search with live suggestions
  • 🎯 Personalized recommendations
  • 🔔 Notifications so you never miss episodes
  • 📚 Clean, organized library system

💡 Built From Scratch

This wasn’t made by a big company or team. I learned everything while building it — from Flutter to Firebase to deploying on Google Play.

Every feature comes from real problems I had myself.

📈 What’s Next

AniFlow is just getting started. I’m constantly improving it based on feedback.

Future updates will bring better recommendations, more customization, and even smarter tracking.
